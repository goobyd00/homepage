Hamantha

Hamantha is a surrealist painter.

She is a Syrian hamster: the largest hamster species, also known to have the frendliest temperment. They're also commonly called teddy bear/golden hamsters, and come in plenty of color ways.

Mr.Whiskers

An aspiring DJ, Mr.Whiskers has an affinity for EDM.

He is a Chinese Dwarf hamster. You can tell by his longer tail and grey color way. He's smaller than the Syrain, but bigger than the rest of the hamster species.

Cool & Beans

They met each other at a skate park.

Cool is a Winter White Dwarf, while Beans is a Russian Dwarf. They're essentially the same, but with different coloration. They also hold the title for 2nd smallest hamster species.

Chris Hamsworth

He dreams of being on the big screen.

Hamsworth is a Roborovski Dwarf, the smallest hamster species. They're known for their eyebrows and skiddish nature.
